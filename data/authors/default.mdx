I am an incoming Ph.D. student in the [CARE Lab](https://polytechnic.purdue.edu/facilities/construction-automation-robotics-and-ergonomics-lab) at **Purdue University**.

Prior to my doctoral studies, I worked as a Machine Learning Engineer for three years, focusing on deep learning-based SLAM, multimodal sensor fusion, and sim-to-real robotic control.

Before entering the industry, I earned my M.S. in Computer Science from the **University of Massachusetts Amherst** and my B.S. in Computer Engineering from the **University of Maryland, College Park**, with an upper-level concentration in machine learning.

My research interests lie at the intersection of machine learning, deep reinforcement learning, and robotic perception.
